What is the difference between var and let?
	Var allows you to redeclare variables and let doesn't
	Var is function scope, let is block scope

What is the difference between var and const?
	Var allows redeclaration of variables and const doesn't

What is the difference between let and const?
	Let allows you to reassign and const doesn't

What is hoisting?
	Variables that are called are used even though they are initialized later in the script.
